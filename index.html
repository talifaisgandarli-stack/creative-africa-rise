Programme Overview

About the Programme

A pan-African programme pairing deep skills training with real entrepreneurship. Running across three delivery formats — a 3-month intensive, a 6-month journey, and a permanent online platform — it builds the next generation of African creative entrepreneurs in digital art, music, fashion, film, AI tools and more.

How the Partnership Works
Expert TeachingAZ practitioners teach, mentor and assess. Curriculum shaped by working industry professionals — not generic educators or textbooks.
Teaching ResidenciesDJ Academy Baku: 1 instructor per cohort residency. BFW + StockIn: 4 online masterclasses/year + 1 in-person workshop per 6-month cohort.
Network AccessParticipants connect directly with AZ partners' professional networks — buyers, industry contacts, creative communities across the Caucasus and Europe.
Community & Mentorship1-on-1 matching with AZ practitioners. Ongoing peer community that stays active well beyond graduation.
Co-CertificationGraduates receive the programme certificate plus a partner-endorsed credential, recognised within AZ professional networks.
Alumni NetworkShared digital community connecting African creatives with AZ experts and collaborators — a lasting professional relationship.
